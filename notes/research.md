# Research notes

Last checked: 2026-07-13

## Planning frame

- Origins: Los Angeles plus Scotland, provisionally Edinburgh/Glasgow.
- Window: October 2026 through January 2027.
- Purpose: team connection with a small amount of substantive work; no conference dependency.
- Preferred character: remote, landscape-led, distinctive, comfortable enough for a mixed team.
- All travel times and scores remain planning estimates until dates, headcount and room policy are known.

## Destination notes

### Big Sur

- Treebones has 16 yurts but states that it is not able to accommodate large groups. It is appropriate only after direct confirmation for a small team.
- Big Sur Lodge is the more group-capable baseline: 61 rooms plus meeting/conference facilities.
- Highway 1 and park access require rechecking before deposit and again before travel.
- Preferred window: late October through the first half of November; later winter dates increase rain and road-risk exposure.

Sources:

- https://www.treebonesresort.com/yurts/
- https://bigsurlodge.com/special-events/groups/
- https://www.parks.ca.gov/AccessibleFeatures/Details/570
- https://www.parks.ca.gov/?page_id=565

### Nuuk

- Direct international access exists from Copenhagen and Keflavík; Icelandair describes Nuuk as a year-round Greenland destination.
- US–Nuuk nonstop service is seasonal and should not be assumed for October–January.
- Visit Greenland notes that fog can cause flight delays. A buffer day is part of the operating concept, not optional padding.
- Hotel Hans Egede has substantial conference/group infrastructure; Hotel Søma is an intimate alternative for roughly 30 participants.
- Preferred window: early October if outdoor/fjord activity matters; November–January is a winter expedition.

Sources:

- https://visitgreenland.com/destinations/nuuk/
- https://visitgreenland.com/plan-your-trip/weather-and-climate/
- https://www.icelandair.com/da-dk/afgange/kampagne/destinations-schedule/
- https://hhe.gl/en/courses-conferences/
- https://traveltrade.visitgreenland.com/providers/nuuk-seamens-home/

### Hudson Valley

- Used as the access and execution benchmark, not necessarily the most memorable choice.
- New York offers the strongest combined schedule depth for LA and Edinburgh; exact nonstop service from each Scottish origin must be checked for the selected dates.
- Property selection should emphasize exclusive-use common space so the experience does not become a hotel stay with meetings.

Source:

- https://www.edinburghairport.com/flights/destinations

### Bintan

- Bintan Resorts describes 19 independent resorts and access by approximately 60-minute catamaran from Singapore.
- Natra Bintan explicitly markets corporate retreats and team-building facilities.
- A Singapore recovery night is recommended before the ferry.

Sources:

- https://www.bintan-resorts.com/about-us/
- https://www.bintan-resorts.com/venue/the-meeting-tent/
- https://www.brf.com.sg/charters-group-booking/

### Madeira

- Included as the weather hedge for November–January.
- It strongly favors Scotland on travel time, so it should be evaluated in an alternating-location fairness model.

Source:

- https://visitmadeira.com/en

### Fogo Island

- Included as a high-impact wildcard, not a default recommendation.
- Newfoundland and Labrador Tourism notes that many tourist sites and campgrounds close after the Canadian Thanksgiving weekend in October, though principal accommodations remain available year-round.

Source:

- https://www.exploretnl.ca/automne-hiver

## Items to verify after narrowing to three

- Exact team headcount and required single rooms.
- Scottish departure airports and acceptable ground positioning.
- Passport and visa/entry requirements for every traveler.
- Door-to-door itineraries on the same candidate dates.
- Property buyout or common-space exclusivity.
- Food restrictions, mobility constraints and cold/weather tolerance.
- Cancellation, force-majeure and transport-disruption terms.
- Ground transport ownership: one operator from airport to property and back.
- Medical access and travel insurance appropriate to remoteness.

## Scoring caveat

Scores in the web artifact are intentionally coarse. Their purpose is to expose preference tradeoffs, not manufacture numerical certainty. Date-specific access and lodging quotes should replace these estimates in the second decision pass.
